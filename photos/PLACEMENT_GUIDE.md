## Layout Notes for AI Agent

1. **Page background:** Light yellow/cream (#F5F0C1 or similar)
2. **Section pattern:** Each project follows: `Divider → Heading (centered) → Divider → Content rows → Divider`
3. **Side-by-side layouts:** When images appear next to text, they share the row — typically images on one side (~30-50% width) and text on the other
4. **Multi-image rows:** Images placed side by side share the same vertical row, with varying widths
5. **All section headings** are styled as large, centered paragraph text (not HTML heading tags) in uppercase
6. **The "download Jolly Bean font" link** is a small text link below the Convergence font specimen image
7. **Dividers** are thin horizontal rules spanning the content width, used to separate sections and sub-sections
8. **Text styling:** Body text appears to be a serif or transitional font, small/medium size, with generous line spacing
