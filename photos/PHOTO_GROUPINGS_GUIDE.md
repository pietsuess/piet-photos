## Layout Notes for AI Agent

1. **Page background:** Black / very dark
2. **Hero images** are full-bleed (edge-to-edge, no padding) with custom heights varying from ~897px to ~1544px
3. **Gallery grids** are always 3 columns wide, no gaps between images. Row counts vary (2, 3, or 8 rows)
4. **The opening collage** (Group 2) is unique вЂ” 3 images in an artistically staggered/overlapping layout, not a uniform grid
5. **The closing image** (Group 18, Dadaab) is NOT full-bleed вЂ” it is inset/centered at ~600px wide
6. **No text labels** appear between sections on the photo page вЂ” the groupings are purely visual
7. **Gallery images** within the same grid share the same aspect ratio (either landscape ~3:2 or square ~1:1)
8. **The "PHOTOGRAPHY" heading** appears twice: once overlaid on the title hero (H3) and once at the bottom in the closing bio section (H2)
